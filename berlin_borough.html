Analysis of Berlin Boroughs

Objective: To analyze Berlin's boroughs based on lifestyle factors (Family-friendliness, Cultural richness, Nightlife opportunities, and Pet-friendliness) while incorporating demographic and crime data.

Data Sources:

  • Amenities dataset: Evaluates borough features (parks, schools, etc.).
  • Demographics dataset: Population, age groups, and foreign residents.
  • Crime dataset: Borough-level crime statistics.

Methodology

Scoring System:

  • Family-friendly: Parks, playgrounds, schools.
  • Cultural: Museums, theaters, galleries.
  • Nightlife: Bars, clubs, and late-night activity venues.
  • Pet-friendly: Parks, pet stores, veterinary clinics.

Adjustments:

  1. Crime Penalties:
    • Penalized Cultural and Nightlife scores for boroughs with higher crime rates.
  2. Safety Rewards:
    • Rewarded Family and Pet scores for boroughs with lower residential burglary rates.

Scoring Formula:

  • Scores were normalized to a 010 scale for consistency.
  • Adjustments based on weighted correlations with crime and demographics.

Demographics and Amenities: Insights

Key Findings: Demographics and Amenities

  1. Age Groups and Amenities:

    • Younger populations (1827) show a strong preference for nightlife-related amenities (bars and theaters).
    • Families with children (under 6) are associated with parks, playgrounds, and schools.

  2. Foreign Residents and Amenities:

    • Foreigners correlate strongly with cultural amenities (museums).

  3. Policy Implications:

    • Boroughs with a younger demographic should focus on expanding nightlife opportunities.
    • Family-dense boroughs should prioritize family-friendly amenities.

    • Increase safety measures in Boroughs 2 and 6 to boost their attractiveness for families.

  2. Safety Interventions:

    • Address high crime rates in Borough 2 to sustain its Cultural and Nightlife appeal.

    • Highlight Borough 2 as a hub for culture and nightlife, targeting younger demographics.

Conclusion

This analysis provides a data-driven overview of Berlins boroughs:

  1. Scores were computed based on amenities, demographics, and crime data.
  2. Adjustments ensured fairness, incorporating safety and livability.
  3. Recommendations aim to assist urban planning and lifestyle-focused policies.

The findings offer actionable insights for both residents and city planners.
